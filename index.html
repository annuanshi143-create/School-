About Our School

Shree Shiv Samaj Kalyan Prathamik Viddyalay, Masona (Azamatgarh), is a temple of learning dedicated to the holistic development of children. We focus on providing quality education from Nursery to Class 8th, blending traditional values with modern digital learning.

Our Mission

Our mission is to nurture young minds in a rural environment, ensuring every child in Azamgarh gets access to the best academic resources, discipline, and creative growth.
